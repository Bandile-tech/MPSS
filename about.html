About Mpelembe

Excellence, Discipline, Innovation — since 1983.

Who We Are

We nurture academic excellence and moral integrity, guided by Christian values. Our holistic curriculum equips students to excel in academics, character, and leadership.

  • Academic Excellence
  • Integrity
  • Respect
  • Service

Our Mission

To provide a holistic education that fosters intellectual growth, moral integrity, and leadership skills — preparing students to contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution of learning in Zambia and beyond, renowned for excellence, innovation, and the nurturing of future leaders rooted in Christian values.

Our History

Founded in 1983, Mpelembe Secondary School has grown from a small Catholic mission school into one of Zambia’s premier secondary institutions. With a tradition of discipline and excellence, we have consistently produced graduates who excel both locally and internationally.

Our Leadership

At Mpelembe, leadership is not only taught — it is lived. Our management team, teachers, and student leaders work together to create a culture of growth, innovation, and service.
