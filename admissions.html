Why Choose Mpelembe?

Academic Excellence

We follow a rigorous curriculum that combines the Cambridge Syllabus with Zambian education standards, producing top-performing graduates.

Faith & Character

As a Catholic institution, we nurture both intellect and character through discipline, service, and integrity.

Supportive Community

Students, parents, and alumni form a strong community guided by Mpelembe’s motto: “Success Through Effort.”

How to Apply

  1. Access the online Application Form: Click here to apply.
  2. Prepare Required Documents: Include your most recent school report, birth certificate, and NRC (if applicable).
  3. Submit Your Application: Deliver the completed form to our office or email it to admissions@mpelembe.edu.zm.
  4. Entrance Assessment: Shortlisted applicants will be invited for interviews and aptitude tests.
  5. Acceptance: Successful candidates will receive offer letters and enrollment details.

Applications are open for both day and boarding students for Grades 8–12.
